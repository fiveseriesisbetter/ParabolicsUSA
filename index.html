Parabolic Career Coaching

Connect with your future, invest in your success

What we do

Parabolic is a career coaching firm for financial services, consulting, and technology full-time and internship jobs. We also use our network to pair startups and entrepreneurs with professional coaches. We provide our clients with services and products that give them a competitive edge. We are not a third party job placement firm.

How we do it

For our career coaching, we create a personalized job recruiting game plan to break into the financial services, consulting, and technology industries. We oversee the entire application and networking process and provide a dedicated team of coaches who are full-time employees in the industries of interest. We also share intellectual property that explains the nuances of job recruiting. For our entrepreneurship coaching services, we validate our coaches in order to provide our clients with the best service.
