About Us

Connect with your future, invest in your success

Who we are

Parabolic LLC is a client-focused career coaching and entrepreneurship advisory firm. Parabolic helps its clients secure high paying front-office internships and full-time jobs in finance, consulting, and technology. Parabolic also provides entrepreneurship coaching to its clients.

At Parabolic, we put our client’s results first. We understand how competitive the industries are to break into, and offer resources to give our clients a clear strategy on how to set themselves apart from other applicants. Furthermore, we connect small businesses and startups to established entrepreneurs in our network.

What sets us apart

We create a personalized game plan to get results and break into the finance and consulting industries. We oversee the entire application and networking process of about 60+ applications seamlessly through our software platform, dedicated team of coaches who are full-time employees in the finance and consulting industry, intellectual property, and commitment to client results.

Our Mission

Parabolic is dedicated to helping students and young professionals get exposure to and navigate the competitive industries of financial services, consulting, technology, and entrepreneurship.

Company Values

Results : The career success of our clients is our top priority. We track the success of our clients and offer payment options contingent on the success of our clients. We believe our services augment our client’s chances of achieving their career objectives. Our network of coaches, intellectual property, network of recruiters, and experience enable our clients to navigate the recruiting process for some of the most competitive and sought-after jobs.
Integrity : Our services and approach are always handled with the utmost transparency, honesty, and fairness. We hold ourselves to high business standards.
Giving back : We believe it is necessary to give back to under-served communities. We offer pro-bono products and services to underrepresented minorities, first-generation students, and low-income students interested in our services.
Client-focused approach : Our motives are always aligned with our clients. We tailor our service and strategy toward each client and take ownership of our clients’ success.

Meet the Team

Originally from New Jersey, Olufemi “Femi” Palmer graduated from the University of Pennsylvania in 2019 with a Bachelor in Economics and is an incoming Associate Consultant at Bain & Company in the New York office. Prior to college, Femi attended the Marine Academy of Technology and Environmental Science and served as the president of an award-winning Math League team while being an all-conference varsity football player.

Femi was heavily involved on campus at the University of Pennsylvania. He spent time serving on elected student government boards, playing varsity football, and networking with Penn’s diverse student body. Through involvement with diversity-focused pre-professional organizations like Sponsors for Educational Opportunity (SEO), Management Leadership for Tomorrow (MLT), and Black Wharton Undergraduate Association (BWUA), Femi was able to learn how to navigate the finance and consulting recruiting landscape.

After his freshman year, Femi interned with Morgan Stanley in a Sales and Trading rotational internship. The following summer, Femi interned in Investment Banking in the Technology, Media, and Telecom group at Credit Suisse as a Douglas Paul Scholar before doing an externship with Bain & Company through the Building Entrepreneurial Leaders program. In Femi’s junior summer, he interned with Bain & Company in the Los Angeles office and received a full-time offer. Femi has also spent time working on award-winning startups including Releaf, a Y-Combinator funded agro-processing company based in Akwa Ibom, Nigeria.

Femi’s experience has helped him receive offers with dozens of finance and consulting firms including Goldman Sachs, JP Morgan, Morgan Stanley, and Bain & Company. Femi has helped several of his peers land internships at competitive finance and consulting firms through his unique approach to networking, interview preparation, company engagement, and resume building.

Outside of work, Femi volunteers his time mentoring high school students in New Jersey and works as an International Sports Sciences Association (ISSA) certified personal trainer. He also enjoys traveling, reading, long walks, sports, and music in his free time.
